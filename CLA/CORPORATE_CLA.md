## 1. Purpose

The organization wishes to permit its personnel to contribute to the Project, and the Project requires licensing terms that support both:

1. public AGPLv3 distribution; and
2. separate commercial/proprietary licensing by the designated sole rights-holder.

## 2. Definitions

- "Project" means the software repository and related materials governed by AGPLv3.
- "Contribution" means any source code, object code, documentation, data, tests, scripts, configuration, or other material intentionally Submitted for inclusion in the Project by an Authorized Contributor.
- "Authorized Contributor" means personnel identified by the organization under Section 6 (Authorized Contributors List).

## 3. Corporate Authority and Representations

Organization represents that:

1. it has sufficient rights and authority to permit Contributions by its Authorized Contributors under this Agreement;
2. execution of this Agreement does not breach any other agreement binding on Organization;
3. it will use reasonable efforts to ensure Authorized Contributors submit only material they are permitted to contribute.
